What is Organic Food?

Organic food is the kind of processed food produced by organic farming methods.
It is grown without artificial chemicals, such as human-made pesticides and fertilizers, and does not contain genetically modified organisms (GMOs).
Organic foods include freshly produced veggies, meats, dairy products inclusive of frozen meals.
The market for organic food has grown substantially recently with distinct production, processing, distribution, and retail systems.
